Description

Wrap yourself in the magic of Christmas! Grab your coloring pens and coziest blanket as you explore the brand-new Cozy Christmas Coloring Book, a special collaboration between bestselling creator Coco Wyo and Penguin Random House. It’s the perfect gift for anyone looking to unwind and enjoy a little holiday cheer.

With simple, cute pages you can complete in one sitting! Picture yourself snuggled up, coloring all sorts of of relaxing scenes -- from snow-covered rooftops, to cozy fireplaces, to steaming mugs of hot cocoa. Cozy Christmas will fill your holidays with color and festive joy.
